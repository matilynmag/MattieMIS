The F1 Race Experience

What You Should Know:

  1. You don't need to have a three day pass to have a great time!
    • The Friday and Saturday events, though fun, are not neeeded if you are going on a budget!
    • Friday includes Free Practice 1 and 2, with the exception of Sprint weekends.
    • Saturday is devoted to qualifying for the Sunday race, with the exception of Sprint weekends.
    • If you are debating not going because you cannot afford the full weekend, go for just the race!
  2. Show up for the driver parade!
    • The parade is before the race and is only ten minutes long! You get to see all your favorite drivers in the flesh!
  3. Participate in the festivities that aren't part of the race.
  4. Prepare for extreme weather conditions.
  5. Take a few videos, but put the phone down and enjoy the race and the atmosphere!

Beatiful Scenes, Incredible Experiences





Monaco

This circuit is arguably the most iconic and well known of all the F1 grid. This street race incorporates several tight corners and proves to be a breathtaking street race full of glamour and fame. It doesn't get much more classy than this.




Singapore

This night race always brings the visuals. On top of that, this race is often met with a shocking result, sometimes the only upset of the season! It almost makes it worth it for most fans to wake up in the middle of the night just to watch!




Vegas

This race had quite the reputation before it even happened. Although fans speculated that it would be all show, it turned out to be an action packes and entertaining race! With spectacular scenes of racing down the strip, and the debut of the sphere in F1 action, the race was very memorable.
